Scaling the human databehind humanoid robots

Figure AI is training humanoid robots on millions of hours of real-world data. Experis builds and runs the human engine behind it: top-of-funnel talent, a proven annotation practice, and embedded AI engineering.

What we heard

The challenge, in full

Before we talk about how we help, here is the problem as you framed it. Training a humanoid robot on real-world data takes a whole stack of things going right. This is our accounting of what has to be true for Figure AI to succeed.

The overarching need
Egocentric data
First-person data captured from wearable cameras and sensors. It is the fuel for human-activity recognition, robotics, and embodied AI, and sourcing it at scale is the whole game.
Scale

Volume, sustained

Millions of hours of real-world data, captured over a multi-year ramp that only grows.

Workforce

People, at the ready

A large, distributed workforce to source, onboard, and manage continuously.

Environment

Out in the world

Capture in everyday, uncontrolled settings across diverse people and places.

Quality

Clean signal

The gains now come from the quality of human-reviewed data. Consistency is the hard part.

Security

Data as crown-jewel IP

Training data that can’t leak, and can’t end up feeding a competitor’s model.

Velocity

Keeping pace

A pipeline that moves at the speed of the model roadmap and its launch timelines.
